Services

Electrical Engineering Services

Electrical Shop Drawings

Preparation of coordinated electrical shop drawings (power, lighting, low current, earthing & lightning) ready for site execution and consultant approval.

Modeling & BIM Coordination

3D modeling and coordination for electrical systems using modern CAD/BIM tools to minimize clashes with architectural, structural, and mechanical works.

Design & Calculations

Complete electrical design including load calculations, cable sizing, short-circuit checks, panel schedules, and single-line diagrams in compliance with IEC/NEC/NFPA/BS.

Bid Pricing & BOQ Support

Preparing BOQ, reviewing quantities, and supporting contractors and clients in pricing, value engineering, and technical queries during tender stage.
